History of Skateboarding

The history of skateboarding first started around the 1950s when kids would attach their roller skate wheels to a wooden plank and would ride it around. By the 70s it started to take off and skateboard companies started appearing and making the things that make up a skateboard. The deck, wheels, trucks and bearings. The first skateparks also opened in california so skaters would have a designated place to skate. In the 1990s vert skating (the skating that is done on a huge half pipe) became popular and new tricks were being developed, one that involved a flip, thats what leads us to our next page
