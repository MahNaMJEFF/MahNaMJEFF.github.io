What is frontside 180

A frontside 180 is a fronside shuvit where your body follows the board when it does a 180

What is needed to frontside 180

The frontside 180 isnt a very hard trick all that is required is how to ollie. A revert is also helpful but not required. this trick is pretty much a ollie just you move your shoulders and your feet will follow.

Step 1: pop the tail and jump Step 2:slide your front foot up the board to the tail and move your shoulders 180 Step 3: stick the landing step 4: ???? step 5: profit!

how to build off the frontside 180

You can always add a kickflip in it and it will be a frontside flip. or you can do a full 360 and do a frontside 360.
