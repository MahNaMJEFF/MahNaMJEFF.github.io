What is pop shuv it

A pop shuv it is a skateboarding trick performed by popping the tail in a scooping motion to make a 180* rotation of the skateboard. it is not known who created the popshuvit but rodney mullen did popularize it.

What is needed to pop shuvit

This trick isnt too hard to get down but some other tricks to know are a shuvit where you dont pop the tail. Just know how to pop the tail hard enough so a good ollie could also help to. You only need to use your back foot with this trick so its god for begginers

Step 1: have your back foot slightly below the pocket of the tail step 2: (optional) have your front foot at a slight angle by the bolts, this just helps get your front foot out of the way Step 3: scoop the tail and jump Step 4: wait for the rotation and land step 5:????? step 6: profit!
