What is kickflip

the kickflip is a 360* flip by kicking your front foot diagonally off the nose of the skateboard. Jumping and landing when the rotation is over. It was invented by rodney mullen in the 1990s when he accidentally kicked out of a ollie and it flipped over

what is needed to kickflip

You are going to have to have a understanding of timing so i would make sure that you have good leveled ollies that are pretty high. If you simplify it this trick is pretty much a ollie just instead of flicking your foot up you flick it diagonally. here are the steps.

Step 1: place your front foot at a 45* angle slightly below the bolts Step 2: pop the tail down like a ollie and jump Step 3: flick your front foot out step 4: land when rotation is over step 5: ????? step 6: profit!
